CV   |   LinkedIn

Research
My current research is focused on unsupervised learning with an emphasis on spatiotemporal representation learning. I am passionate about applying machine learning methods in computational photography as well as in scientific domains like climate science. My interests are in studying how large-scale spatiotemporal data with complex correlations and high dimensionality affect the representation and thus the generalization of machine learning models.
