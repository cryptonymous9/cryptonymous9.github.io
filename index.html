About
My name is Nidhin Harilal and I am third-year Ph.D. student in computer science at the University of Colorado Boulder, where I am advised by Dr. Claire Monteleoni. I am recipient of Awtar and Teji Singh Fellowship. Prior to my Ph.D, I recieved my Bachelorâ€™s degree with honours in Computer Science & Engineering in 2021 from IIT Gandhinagar.

My research interests broadly lie in machine learning, with a focus on unsupervised representation learning. I want to study how large-scale spatiotemporal data with complex correlations and high dimensionality affect the representation and thus the generalization of machine learning models. In the past, I have focused on improving the spatiotemporal resolution of climate datasets for enhanced prediction, alongside projects on generating high-dynamic-range videos and image captioning. Recently, I am working on harnessing Generative AI to advance modern machine learning techniques. I also care about deep learning robustness and interpretability.
